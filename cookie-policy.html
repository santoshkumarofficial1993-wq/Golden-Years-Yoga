Cookie Policy

Last updated: 2026. This page explains how and why we use cookies on this Site.

What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. They help the site work, remember your choices, and understand how it is used. Some cookies are set by us ("first-party") and some by third parties such as advertising and analytics providers ("third-party").

Types of cookies we use

Strictly necessary

Required for the Site to function (for example, security and basic navigation). These cannot be switched off in our systems.

Performance & analytics

Help us understand which pages are popular and how visitors move around the Site, so we can improve it. For example, Google Analytics.

Advertising

Used by us and by third-party vendors, including Google, to show relevant ads and limit how often you see them. Google and its partners may use advertising cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this and other websites. See our Privacy Policy for details.

Managing cookies

  • You can control and delete cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers let you block or remove cookies — search your browser's help for "cookies".
  • You can opt out of personalised Google advertising at Google Ads Settings.
  • You can opt out of many vendors at aboutads.info/choices or youronlinechoices.eu.

Please note that blocking some cookies may affect how parts of the Site work.

Changes

We may update this Cookie Policy as our practices or the law change. Updates appear on this page with a revised date.
