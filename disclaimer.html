Health Disclaimer

Please read this carefully. Your safety matters more to us than any exercise.

Not medical advice. The information, illustrations and routines on Golden Years Yoga are provided for general educational purposes only. They are not a substitute for professional medical advice, diagnosis or treatment.

Consult a professional first

Always seek the advice of your physician, physiotherapist or another qualified health provider before beginning any exercise programme, and especially if you have a medical condition, are pregnant, take medication that affects your balance or blood pressure, are recovering from surgery or injury, or have any concerns about whether an activity is suitable for you. Never disregard professional medical advice or delay seeking it because of something you have read on this Site.

Listen to your body

  • Move slowly and stay within a comfortable, pain-free range of motion.
  • Stop immediately and seek medical attention if you experience chest pain, severe shortness of breath, dizziness, faintness, or sharp or sudden pain.
  • Use a sturdy chair and a safe, clear, non-slip space.
  • Do not attempt any movement that feels unsafe for your body on a given day.

Assumption of risk

Any physical activity carries some risk of injury. By using the routines and information on this Site, you voluntarily accept and assume those risks. You are responsible for exercising within your own limits.

No liability

To the fullest extent permitted by law, Golden Years Yoga and its contributors accept no responsibility or liability for any injury, loss or damage incurred as a result of using, or relying on, any information or routine provided on this Site. Please also see our Terms of Use.

In an emergency

If you believe you are experiencing a medical emergency, call your local emergency number immediately. Do not rely on this Site for urgent care.

Questions

If anything here is unclear, please contact us before starting.
