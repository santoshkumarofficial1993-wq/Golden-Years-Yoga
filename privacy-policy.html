Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026. This policy explains what information we collect and how we use and protect it.

Golden Years Yoga ("we", "us", "our") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, disclose and safeguard your information when you visit goldenyearsyoga.com (the "Site"). By using the Site, you agree to the practices described here. This is a general template; please have it reviewed for your jurisdiction (for example, GDPR in the EU/UK or CCPA in California) before relying on it.

1. Information we collect

  • Information you give us. When you contact us or sign up for our newsletter, you may provide your name, email address and the content of your message.
  • Information collected automatically. Like most websites, we (and our service providers) may automatically collect technical data such as your IP address, browser type, device information, pages visited and the date and time of your visit, using cookies and similar technologies.

2. How we use your information

  • To respond to your enquiries and provide the resources you request;
  • To send our newsletter and free guide, where you have asked us to;
  • To understand how the Site is used so we can improve it;
  • To display advertising that helps keep our content free;
  • To maintain the security and proper functioning of the Site.

3. Cookies and similar technologies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device. We use them to remember your preferences, measure traffic, and serve advertising. You can control or delete cookies through your browser settings, and you can refuse non-essential cookies. For more detail, see our Cookie Policy.

4. Advertising & third-party vendors (including Google)

To keep our guidance free, this Site may display advertising provided by third-party vendors and ad networks, including Google.

  • Third-party vendors, including Google, use cookies to serve ads based on a user's prior visits to this and other websites.
  • Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads to you based on your visit to our Site and/or other sites on the Internet.
  • You may opt out of personalised advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings.
  • You can opt out of a third-party vendor's use of cookies for personalised advertising by visiting www.aboutads.info or youronlinechoices.eu.

For more information about how Google uses data when you use our partners' sites or apps, see Google's Privacy & Terms.

5. Analytics

We may use analytics services (such as Google Analytics) to understand how visitors use the Site. These services collect information sent by your browser, including pages visited, and use it to compile reports on Site activity.

6. How we share information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share it with service providers who help us operate the Site (such as email, hosting and advertising partners), or where required by law. These providers are only permitted to use your information to provide services to us.

7. Your rights & choices

Depending on where you live, you may have the right to access, correct, delete or restrict the use of your personal information, and to object to certain processing. To exercise any right, email us at contact@goldenyearsyoga.com. You can unsubscribe from our newsletter at any time using the link in any email.

8. Data retention & security

We keep personal information only for as long as needed for the purposes described here, then delete or anonymise it. We use reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your information, though no method of transmission over the Internet is completely secure.

9. Children's privacy

This Site is intended for adults. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 16. If you believe a child has provided us information, please contact us and we will delete it.

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes take effect when posted on this page, with the "Last updated" date revised accordingly.

11. Contact us

Questions about this policy? Email contact@goldenyearsyoga.com or use our contact page.
